Process

Empathize

Learn more about the audience and understand the context of use.

Define

Define project scope and requiremnts. Create a point of view that's based on the users' need and insights.

Ideate

Create UX artifacts and solutions such as the structure (flow), skeleton (wireframes) and surface (UI & Style).

Prototype and test

Put ideas in front of users, get feedback, refine the design and repeat the process until we are happy with the result.
